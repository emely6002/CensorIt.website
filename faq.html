Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to common questions.

Will CensorIt affect stream performance? Will it cause a delay?

CensorIt will cause a slight delay in your stream, but it will not affect the quality of the stream. The delay is usually around 5 seconds.

How long does installation usually take?

Installation is quick and easy. From downloading the app to streaming with it, it takes around 5 minutes.

Will CensorIt leak my stream data?

CensorIt doesn't leak any stream data by any of its users.

Does CensorIt work in different countries?

Unfortunately, CensorIt currently only works on license plates in North America. But we are currently working on expanding it across different countries!

How much does CensorIt cost?

CensorIt is absolutely free for everyone to use! Just follow the steps on the How to Install Page.
