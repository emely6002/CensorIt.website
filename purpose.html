Purpose

CensorIt exists to make policy enforceable. Teams should not have to choose between moving fast and staying compliant—rules should be clear, consistent, and easy to audit when questions come up.

Outcome First

Every feature ties back to outcomes you can measure: fewer incidents, faster review, and less manual triage.

Protection

Our goal is to prioritize the protection of the sensitive material of the individuals that have not consented to be in the stream.

Trust and transparency

Moderation is sensitive work. CensorIt is built with clear logging, role-based access, and defaults that favor explainability.
