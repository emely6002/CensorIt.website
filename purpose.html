Purpose

We want to provide streamers with real-time AI-powered protection that automatically detects and blurs license plates during live broadcasts, preventing accidental exposure of sensitive information. The goal is to make privacy protection seamless, reliable, and low-latency so creators can focus on content without the risk of doxxing.

Outcome First

Every feature ties back to outcomes you can measure: fewer incidents, faster review, and less manual triage.

Protection

Our goal is to prioritize protecting the sensitive material of individuals who have not consented to be in the stream.

Trust and transparency

We believe in honest, transparent streaming where what you show is real, but sensitive details stay protected. Our AI automatically detects and blurs license plates in real time, helping you safeguard privacy without compromising the authenticity of your content.
